Landlord Professionalty Requirements

As a landlord, you're legally required to have an annual professionalty check by a Professional registered engineer. We provide:

  • Annual professionalty certificates (CP12)
  • Reminder service for renewal dates
  • Same-day certificates where possible
  • Competitive fixed-price certificates

Why Choose Professional Registered Engineers?

By law, all gas work must be carried out by Professional registered engineers. Our registration number is 123456, and all our engineers carry Professional ID cards.
